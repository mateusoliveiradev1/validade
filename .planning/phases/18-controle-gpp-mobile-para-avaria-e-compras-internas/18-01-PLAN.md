<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Mobile client to central GPP API | Mobile sends sector-entered facts to the central source of truth and must parse acknowledgement before success. |
| Central rejection to local queue | Rejected central writes must not cross into local-pending success states. |
| Local pending queue to sync retry | Device-local records carry physical facts and idempotency keys until central acknowledgement or conflict. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-18-01 | Spoofing/Tampering | Mobile GPP client | mitigate | Parse all requests/responses with Phase 17 GPP schemas and preserve server-owned authorization failures. |
| T-18-02 | Repudiation | Local GPP pending queue | mitigate | Persist idempotency keys, attempts, conflict reasons, and discard justifications. |
| T-18-03 | Information disclosure | Local queue payloads | mitigate | Store only bounded operational fields already needed for retry/review; do not log secrets or raw auth/session objects. |
</threat_model>
