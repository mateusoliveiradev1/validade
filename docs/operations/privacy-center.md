# Privacy Center Operations

The Privacy Center is a pilot trust surface, not legal advice. Before operating with real people, the organization must configure the responsible channel/encarregado and its internal handling process outside the public repository.

## Required Sections

1. Politica de Privacidade
2. Termos de Uso
3. Seguranca da conta
4. Permissoes do aparelho
5. Dados usados pelo app
6. Canal/encarregado
7. Solicitacao de direitos LGPD

## Operational Data Explained

The app uses identity, store, role, physical actions, lots, tasks, evidence metadata, timestamps, audit history, sync state, and device permissions to direct and confirm operational work. It does not require sales, inventory, supplier, or forecasting data for the pilot.

## Rights Requests

- The app records an authenticated request for access, correction, deletion, portability, processing information, consent revocation, processing objection, or automated-decision review.
- The organization must route the request to its designated channel/encarregado, verify identity according to its policy, and record the response outside the public repository.
- Do not paste private evidence, signed links, object keys, credentials, or real personal data into an issue, commit, test fixture, or this runbook.

## Device Permissions

Camera and notification permissions explain their operational purpose. When a manual path exists, denying a permission does not silently claim completion; the operator sees the required next step or justification.
